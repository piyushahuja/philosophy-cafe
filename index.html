Penny University

At Penny University, we discuss ideas developed as answers to some of the most profound questions in philosophical and political thought

Our salons are a way for people to exchange ideas and inspire each other in their personal, professional and creative lives. In a world of high university fees and economic inequality, our discussions offers an conversational learning space to people from all levels of society. Anyone who can afford a coffee is able to join in.

The "coffee-as-entry-price" is in keeping up with the tradition of medieval coffeehouses ("penny universities"), which spurred creativity and innovation during the age of enlightenment in Europe. The donations help pay for the server, domain and other maintenance costs.
