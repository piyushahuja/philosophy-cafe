Penny University

At Penny University, we discuss questions that are timeless and universal. It offers an alternative form of learning to structured academic learning.

Our salons are meant for people to exchange ideas and inspire each other in their personal, professional and creative lives. In a world of high university fees and economic inequality, our discussions invite people from all levels of society. Anyone who can afford a coffee is able to join in.

We ask for the price of a coffee for our conversation salons. This is in keeping up with the tradition of medieval coffeehouses ("penny universities"), which spurred creativity and innovation during the age of enlightenment in Europe.
