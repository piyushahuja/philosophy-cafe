Penny University

At Penny University, we discuss answers to some of the most relevant questions in philosophical and political thought.

Our salons are a way for people to be engage with important ideas and get inspiration in their own personal, professional and creative lives. In a world of high university fees and economic inequality, our discussions offers a conversational learning space to people from all levels of society. Anyone who can afford a coffee is able to join in.
